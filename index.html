Popular Destination Around Us

Jampore beach is one of the cleanest beaches in Daman. It attracts its visitors with tranquil environment and serene views. It is mainly known for numerous adventurous activities, enthralling views of the widespread Arabian sea and a peaceful environment to give you a break from the bustling life of the city. It also has some of the best eating points and beach snacks where you can enjoy refreshing drinks and scrumptious food.

Moti Daman fort was built by the Portuguese in the sixteenth century. It has an expansive courtyard and a gateway adorned with beautiful sculpture. It is one of the best tourist attractions in Daman. It comprises impressive colonial style architecture with ten bastions, two gateways, huge walls and Iberian church.

Cathedral of Bom Jesus is a magnificent Roman Catholic Cathedral made as a center of devotion for the Christians as well as followers of other religions. It has magnificent altarpieces and the intricately carved doorway to enter the church and feel like God's own home. It is a 400 years old church with ornamental form architecture seen rarely in any other monument making it one of the best churches to visit while in Daman. Mass Prayers are offered every weekend with the hope to wipe away the suffering of people.
